IRS Form 1065 Schedule K-1 (2018) - Partner’s Share of Income, Deductions, Credits, etc.
Suggest Edits

This form is issued for all entities in a partnership to report each share of earnings, losses, deductions, and/or credits. This form is issued by taxpayers who are investors in limited partnerships (LPs) and, in some cases, exchange-traded funds (ETFs).

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1065_SCHEDULE_K_1_2018 in the form_type parameter.
