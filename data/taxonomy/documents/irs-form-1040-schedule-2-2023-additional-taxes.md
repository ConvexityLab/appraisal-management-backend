IRS Form 1040 Schedule 2 (2023) - Additional Taxes
Suggest Edits

This form is used to calculate and address the Alternative Minimum Tax (AMT) to ensure taxpayer's compliance with this specific tax requirement. It is also used for the repayment of excess premium tax credits received by taxpayers.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_2_2023 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE 2

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_2_2023 supports data capture from the IRS 1040 Schedule 2 only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
