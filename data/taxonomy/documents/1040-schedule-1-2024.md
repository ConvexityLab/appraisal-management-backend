1040 Schedule 1 (2024) - Additional Income and Adjustments to Income
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report additional income such as business or rental income and adjustments to income like student loan interest deductions.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_1_2024 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about processing this document type, see process 1040s.

SCHEDULE 1

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_1_2024 supports data capture from the 1040 Schedule 1 only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
