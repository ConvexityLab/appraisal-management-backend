1120S Schedule M-3 - Net Income (Loss) Reconciliation for S Corporations with Total Assets of $10 Million or more
Suggest Edits

The S corporations with total assets of $10 million or more use this form to reconcile financial statement income (book income) with taxable income.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1120_S_SCHEDULE_M_3 in the form_type parameter.
