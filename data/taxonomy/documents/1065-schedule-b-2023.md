This form is used to provide additional information about a partnership’s ownership structure, financial interests, and foreign transactions as required on Form 1065 (U.S. Return of Partnership Income).

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1065_SCHEDULE_B_2023 in the form_type parameter.
