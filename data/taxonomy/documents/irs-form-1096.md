IRS Form 1096 - Annual Summary and Transmittal of U.S. Information Returns
Suggest Edits

Form 1096 is used to summarize information that is being transmitted on another form to the IRS by the filer. Make sure to submit the most recent Form 1096 (2019).

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use TAX_1096 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about this document processing, see processing 1096 forms.
