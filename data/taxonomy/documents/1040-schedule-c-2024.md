1040 Schedule C (2024) - Profit or Loss from Business
Suggest Edits

This document is used to report income or loss from a business the filer operated or a profession the filer practiced as a sole proprietor. An activity qualifies as a business if:

The filer's primary purpose for engaging in the activity is for income or profit.
The filer is involved in the activity with continuity and regularity.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_C_2024 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE C

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_C_2024 supports data capture from the 1040 Schedule C only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
