1004 - Uniform Residential Appraisal Report
Suggest Edits

Form 1004 contains the information needed for completing a full appraisal of a property. It uses three primary approaches to determine value: cost, sales comparison, and income approaches.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use 1004 in the form_type parameter.
