2555 - Foreign Earned Income

This form is an IRS form that U.S. citizens and resident aliens use to claim the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion, which allows them to exclude a certain amount of foreign-earned income from their taxable income.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_2555 in the form_type parameter.
