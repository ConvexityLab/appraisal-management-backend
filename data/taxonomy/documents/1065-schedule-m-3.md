1065 Schedule M-3 - Net Income (Loss) Reconciliation for Certain Partnerships

This form is used by certain partnerships to provide a detailed reconciliation of financial accounting net income (or loss) to taxable income (or loss).

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1065_SCHEDULE_M_3 in the form_type parameter.
