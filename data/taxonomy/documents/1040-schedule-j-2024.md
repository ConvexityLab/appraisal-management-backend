1040 Schedule J (2024) - Income Averaging for Farmers and Fishermen
Suggest Edits

This form is used to allow taxpayers engaged in the trade or business of farming or fishing to make an election. It helps them calculate their income tax by averaging, over the previous three years (referred to as the base years), a portion or the entirety of their taxable income from farming or fishing.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_J_2024 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE J

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_J_2024 supports data capture from the 1040 Schedule J only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
