1120 (2024) - U.S. Corporation Income Tax Return
Suggest Edits

This form is used by corporations to report various financial details, including balance sheets, reconciliation of income, and analysis of partners' capital accounts for partnerships. This form serves the purpose of calculating and reporting the income tax liability of a corporation to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). Corporations must use Form 1120 to fulfill their tax obligations and provide a comprehensive overview of their financial activities for the tax year.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1120_2024 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about this document processing, see the processing 1120 form.
