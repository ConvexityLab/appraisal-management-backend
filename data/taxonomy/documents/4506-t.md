IRS Form 4506-T - Request for Transcript of Tax Return
Suggest Edits

IRS Form 4506-T, also known as a Request for Transcript of Tax Return, is used to request tax return (W-2, 1099 Transcripts, etc.) information for a particular individual or entity.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_4506_T in the form_type parameter.
