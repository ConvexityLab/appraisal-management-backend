4562 - Depreciation and Amortization (Including Information on Listed Property)
Suggest Edits

This form is used to claim deductions for the depreciation and amortization of property or assets placed in service during the tax year. It also allows taxpayers to claim the Section 179 deduction for qualifying property and report depreciation on listed property.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_4562 in the form_type parameter.
