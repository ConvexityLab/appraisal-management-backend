IRS Form 1040 Schedule SE (2020) - Self Employment Tax
Suggest Edits

IRS Form 1040 Schedule SE is a worksheet that self-employed individuals use to calculate and report the self-employment tax they owe on their earnings. This tax primarily covers Social Security and Medicare taxes for self-employed individuals, typically paid by employees and employers in traditional employment situations.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_SE_2020 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE SE

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_SE_2020 supports data capture from the IRS 1040 Schedule SE only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
