VOE (1005) - Request for Verification of Employment
Suggest Edits

This document serves as a formal request to verify a loan applicant's employment status and income history. It is typically sent to the applicant's current or past employers to confirm details such as the position held, length of employment, and salary information of the individual.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use 1005 in the form_type parameter.
