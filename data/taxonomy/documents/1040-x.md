IRS Form 1040-X - Amended U.S. Individual Income Tax Return
Suggest Edits

This is also known as the Amended US Individual Income Tax Return Form, is used by taxpayers when changes need to be made to already filed income tax documentation. It is used to submit corrections to IRS Forms 1040, 1040A, 1040NR, and 1040NR-EZ.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_X in the form_type parameter.
