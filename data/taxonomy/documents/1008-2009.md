1008 (2009) - Uniform Underwriting and Transmittal Summary
Suggest Edits

This is a standardized form used in the mortgage lending industry. This document summarizes key information needed for the underwriting and approval process of a mortgage loan application. It includes details about the borrower, the mortgage loan they are applying for, the property being purchased or refinanced, and the loan's underwriting recommendation.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use 1008_2009 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about processing this document, click here.
