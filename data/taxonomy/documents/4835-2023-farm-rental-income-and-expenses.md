4835 (2023) - Farm Rental Income and Expenses
Suggest Edits

Form 4835 (2023) is an Internal Revenue Service (IRS) form used by farmers to report income and expenses from farming activities, specifically for reporting farm rental income based on crops or livestock produced by the tenant. It allows farmers to detail their income and expenses related to the rental of farmland.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_4835_2023 in the form_type parameter.
