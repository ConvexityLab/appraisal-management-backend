IRS Form 1040 Schedule R (2021) - Credit for the Elderly or the Disabled
Suggest Edits

IRS Form 1040 Schedule R (2018), also known as the Credit for the Elderly or the Disabled, is used to file for a special credit to older taxpayers. This tax break allows individuals and couples to reduce the amount of their income tax by their allowable credit.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_R_2021 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE R

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_R_2021 supports data capture from the IRS 1040 Schedule R only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
