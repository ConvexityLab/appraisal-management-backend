IRS Form 1120S (2019) - U.S. Income Tax Return for an S Corporation
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report the income, gains, losses, deductions, credits, and other relevant financial information of a domestic corporation or eligible entity that has elected to be treated as an S corporation for tax purposes. This form is essential for S corporations to fulfill their tax obligations and report their financial activities to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). S corporations pass their income, deductions, and credits through to their shareholders, who then report these items on their tax returns.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1120S_2019 in the form_type parameter.
