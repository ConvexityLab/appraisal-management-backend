IRS Form 1040 Schedule E (2021) - Supplemental Income and Loss
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report income or losses stemming from various sources including rental real estate properties, royalties, partnerships, S corporations, estates, trusts, and residual interests in real estate mortgage investment conduits (REMICs).

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_E_2021 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE E

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_E_2021 supports data capture from the IRS 1040 Schedule E only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
