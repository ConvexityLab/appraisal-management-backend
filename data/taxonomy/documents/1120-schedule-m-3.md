1120 Schedule M-3 - Net Income (Loss) Reconciliation for Corporations with Total Assets of $10 Million or more
Suggest Edits

Corporations with $10 million or more in total assets use this form to reconcile financial statement income (book income) with taxable income reported on the tax return.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1120_SCHEDULE_M_3 in the form_type parameter.
