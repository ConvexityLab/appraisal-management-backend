1032 - One-Unit Residential Appraisal Field Review Report
Suggest Edits

This is a specialized document used within the real estate and mortgage lending industries to conduct a thorough review of an initial appraisal report for a single-family home or one-unit residential property. This report is completed by a second appraiser who evaluates the accuracy and validity of the original appraisal by physically inspecting the property and reviewing the initial appraiser’s findings. The field review focuses on verifying the home's condition, size, features, and comparability of the selected comparables used in the initial appraisal.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use 1032 in the form_type parameter.
