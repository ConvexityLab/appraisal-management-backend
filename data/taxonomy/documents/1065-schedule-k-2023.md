1065 Schedule K (2023) - Partners’ Distributive Share Items
Suggest Edits

This document is used for partnerships to report each partner's share of the partnership's income, deductions, credits, and other financial items. This form is submitted as part of the partnership's tax filing and provides a detailed breakdown of how the partnership's financial activities are distributed among its partners.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1065_SCHEDULE_K_2023 in the form_type parameter.
