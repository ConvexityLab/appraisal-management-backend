1040 Schedule F (2024) - Profit or Loss from Farming
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report farm income and expenses. Farmers and ranchers use this form to provide a detailed account of their agricultural activities, including the income generated from farming operations and the associated expenses incurred during the tax year.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_F_2024 in the form_type parameter.

SCHEDULE F

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_F_2024 supports data capture from the 1040 Schedule F only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
