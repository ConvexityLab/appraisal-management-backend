1003 (2020) - Uniform Residential Loan Application
Suggest Edits

The 1003 form is ICE's form number for the Uniform Residential Loan Application (URLA). It details the loan terms and information about the borrower, like income, employment, assets, and expenses. It also requests information that the U.S. government regulates.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use 1003_2020 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about processing this document, click here.
