1040 Schedule 8812 (2024) - Credits for Qualifying Children and Other Dependents
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report the Additional Child Tax Credit. This credit provides additional financial assistance to eligible taxpayers who have qualifying children and may result in a refund even if they do not owe any federal income tax.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_1040_SCHEDULE_8812_2024 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about processing this document type, see process 1040s.

SCHEDULE 8812

The document type A_1040_SCHEDULE_8812_2024 supports data capture from the 1040 Schedule 8812 only. The first two pages of the document 1040 are processed as a separate document type.
