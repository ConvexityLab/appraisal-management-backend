6252 - Installment Sale Income
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report income from an installment sale, allowing taxpayers to spread the recognition of capital gains over several years as payments are received. This form helps calculate the portion of the gain that should be included in taxable income each year.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_6252 in the form_type parameter.
