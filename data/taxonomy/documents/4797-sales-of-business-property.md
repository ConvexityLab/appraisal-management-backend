4797 - Sales of Business Property
Suggest Edits

This form is used to report the sale or exchange of business property, including property used for investment or trade, and to calculate any resulting gains or losses. It also covers the recapture of depreciation and other specific types of dispositions.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use A_4797 in the form_type parameter.
