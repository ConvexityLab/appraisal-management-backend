1003 (2009) - Uniform Residential Loan Application
Suggest Edits

The 1003, also known as the Uniform Residential Loan Application, is a standard form in the mortgage industry used by lenders for those applying for a mortgage loan. It is the primary tool used in the mortgage process.

To use the Upload PDF endpoint for this document, you must use 1003_2009 in the form_type parameter. To learn more about processing this document, click here.
